Naiki Ki Tokri

We are present in 30 cities across all four provinces of Pakistan and our total count of operational tandoors are now at 126, where approximately 7,000 free rotis & naans are distributed on a daily basis.
  • Lahore | 47 Tandoors
  • Rawalpindi | 16 Tandoors
  • Islamabad | 12 Tandoors
  • Karachi | 7 Tandoors
  • Peshawar | 4 Tandoors
  • Sialkot | 3 Tandoors
  • Sadiqabad | 3 Tandoors
  • ...and 23 more cities and growing.

Naiki Ka Dastarkhwan

A project where free cooked meals are distributed consistently on a weekly basis to those in need.

Naiki Ka Thela

Additionally a project has been start that provides free cooked meals through pushcarts on a weekly basis, currently at 5 locations across Pakistan

Naiki Ka Ration

Project has also been started that provides packed rations to the vulnerable population of Pakistan

Naiki Ki Sabeel

The Naiki Ki Sabeel project is a water distribution project that aims to provide water for those in need during this intense summer which is depleting precious water resources and making thousands vulnerable.
