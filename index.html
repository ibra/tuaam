Heres a city-wise breakup of Tandoors

We are present in 30 cities across all four provinces of Pakistan and our total count of operational tandoors are now at 126, where approximately 7,000 free rotis & naans are distributed on a daily basis.

  • Lahore | 47 Tandoors
  • Rawalpindi | 16 Tandoors
  • Islamabad | 12 Tandoors
  • Karachi | 7 Tandoors
  • Peshawar | 4 Tandoors
  • Sialkot | 3 Tandoors
  • Sadiqabad | 3 Tandoors
  • ...and 23 more cities and growing.

A Detailed Overview of The Project

The Tuaam Foundation was started on 27th Ramdan ul Mubarik (20 May 2020), established as a nonprofit organization aiming to feed the most vulnerable population of Pakistan.
