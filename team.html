BOARD OF DIRECTORS

Muhammad Hisham

Mr. Muhammad Hisham is a senior executive at a financial institute. He holds MBA and Mphil degrees and advance diploma qualifiaction from the UK. He has over 22 years of practical experience in the corporate sector and is the co-founder of the Tuaam-E-Miskeen Foundation. He is passionate about poverty alleviation in Pakistan.

Saim bin Saeed

Mr. Saim Bin Saeed is an entrepreneur. He has over 23 years of experience running successful business projects. He is a director at Sitara Peroxide and CEO and MD at Honda Express Pvt Ltd and Director at Honda Avenue. He has attended various training programs locally and internationally.

Syed Awais Naqvi

Syed Awais Naqvi has 5 years of corporate expereince. He holds a M Com degree and is presently working in the financial sector. He is also the Secretary General of TMF and is deeply passionate about philanthropy and social work.

Ameer Hamza Khan Niazi

Mr. Ameer Hamza is an entrepeneur.He is a director at Howdy Pakistan and holds a MBA degree. He has over 10 years of corporate experience with a large telecom company.

Muhammad Sohaib

Mr. Sohaib Sheikh is a corporate lawyer by profession. He has done his LLM from the University of Manchester in the UK. He has over 20 years of experience. He has worked in SECP for 7 years as registrar. He is also a director at Zawiya Academy Rawalpindi. His areas of interest include elimination of hunger and poverty and improving the standard of education in Pakistan.

Haroon Malik

Mr Haroon Ahmed Malik is a senior executive at a big Textile business in Pakistan. He has over 20 years of practical experience with over 15 years being with ICI Pakistan. He holds a MBA degree.
